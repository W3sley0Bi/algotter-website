Notiord Basic Functionalities

Notification

Receive notifications from a Notion board to your Discord channel, keeping you updated on changes. Notification Plus is coming soon.

Search

Look for issues and tickets directly from Discord.

Comment

Write a quick comment on a ticket when you receive a notification.

Create (Beta only)

Create new issues and tickets directly from a Discord channel.

Update (Beta only)

Update existing issues directly from Discord.

Otter AI (Alpha)

Chat with Otter AI and start chatting with you Notion Workspace and Discord Server
